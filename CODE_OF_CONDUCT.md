# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We want DepGraph to be a respectful, professional project for maintainers, contributors, and users.

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- being respectful in disagreements
- assuming good intent
- giving technical feedback clearly and directly
- focusing on what improves the project

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment or personal attacks
- discriminatory language or behavior
- bad-faith disruption
- publishing private information without permission

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to project spaces, issue discussions, pull requests, and other community interactions related to DepGraph.

## Enforcement

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainers at `sara@synthesissoftworks.com`.

All reports will be reviewed and handled reasonably and in good faith.
